Deriv

Deriv[disp, vox]
calculates the derivative of the displacement along the three main axes. disp is the displacement field, vox is the voxel size.

Deriv[disp, vox, mask]
calculates the derivative of the displacement along the three main axes. Sharp edges between the background en disp are solved by the mask. mask is a mask delining the edge of the displacement field.
