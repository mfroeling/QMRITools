SequencePulseAcquire

SequencePulseAcquire[din, H]
performs a pulsaquire experiment of the spin system din given the hamiltonian H with a 90 Degree pulse.

SequencePulseAcquire[din, H, b$1]
performs a pulsaquire experiment of the spin system din given the hamiltonian H with a 90 Degree pulse and b1. The output is a new spinsystem dout.
