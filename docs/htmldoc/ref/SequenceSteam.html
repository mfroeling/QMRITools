SequenceSteam

SequenceSteam[din, H, {te, tm}]
performs a stimulated echo experiment with echo time te and mixing time tm of the spin system din given the hamiltonian H with 3 90 Degree pulses. The te and tm are defined in ms. The output is a new spinsystem dout.
